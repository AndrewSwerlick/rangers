# Overview 

## The Navigator
- Prep Bonus: Trailblazer - Create 2 new trail connections during prep
- Crisis Safe Bonus: Bushwack - Off trail movement is a safe action, and you can perform it so long as you have less than 2 injuries 
- Crisis Costly Bonus: Emergency Extraction - Can take a harm to move one ranger with them off trail
- Desperation Special Move: "I Know a Shortcut" - Spend 1 token, teleport to anylocation on the map, even an occupied one.

## The Hunter
- Prep Bonus: Marksman training - Spend 1 prep token to give everyone advantadge on any attack role. Each ranger can only use this ability once
- Crisis Safe Bonus: Clean Shot - When ambushing a wave token, deal 1 extra damage
- Crisis Costly Bonus: Surpressing Fire - Wave tokens cannot assualt your position next turn, or advance past you, take one harm.
- Desperation special move: "Bullseye at 600 yards" - Spend 1 token, deal 3 momentum damage to any token that's within 2 segments of your location

## The Rigger 
- Prep Bonus: Can place 1 free trap without spending tokens
- Crisis Safe Bonus: Targeted Detonation - If a wave triggers a trap adjactent to your location, add + 1 damage
- Crisis Costly Bonus: Controlled Burn - May start a fire to temporarily block advancement along a road for 2 turns, and deal 1 momentum damage each turn to nearby tokens. Take 1 harm
- Desperation special move: "Scorched Earth" - Spend 2 tokens, permanently destroy a road segment and do 2 momentum damage for each adjacent token 

## The Sentinal
- Prep Bonus: Can place 1 free barricade
- Crisis Safe Bonus: Stalwart Defense - If you hold at a location, roll your next breach defense with advantadge
- Crisis Costly Bonus: Parting Shot - Do an additional +1 damage as you retrat from a location or barricade. Take one harm
- Desperation special move: "Not one Step" - Spend 2 tokens, the next assault on your position fails automatically

## The Medic
- Prep Bonus: Emergency Supplies - During the prep phase, create 2 emergency supply caches. Rangers can move to a stash to recover 1 harm
- Crisis Safe Bonus: Patch Up - Spend your recovery tokens on another ranger to remove 2 harm during the recovery phase 
- Crisis Costly Bonus: Dig - Take one exhaustion, get an extra recovery token
- Desperation special move: "Nobody Dies Today" - Spend 2 tokens, prevent a ranger from being captured

## Commander 
- Prep Bonus: Pick another ranger, and exercise their prep bonus a second time 
- Crisis Safe Bonus:  Inspire - As an action, grant adjacent ranger advantage on next roll
- Crisis Costly Bonus: No One Gets Left Behind - When adjacent ranger fails a roll take 1 exhaustion to turn it into partial success (4-5 result)
- Desperation special move: "FOR THE RANGERS!" - Spend 2 tokens, all rangers get advantage on their next roll
